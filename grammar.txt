# GRAMMAR

adjectives after nouns 
subject verb object word order

auxilliary verbs (can, etc) = placed before verbs (i can make = n'tu prata fari)
adverbs: adjectives after verbs (arr hypothicus = hypothetically steal)

place where verb happens = "place verb" (locas comprande = place of understanding)
this can be extended to situation where verb happens (locas resis = fight)

impersonal verbs ("it is snowing", etc) = "verb happen" (glazis ake = snow happen / it is snowing)
